Tomato soup

Description

Hot and fresh tomato soup that will keep you warm during the cold winter days!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of chopped fresh tomatoes
  • 2/3 cup chicken broth
  • 1/3 chopped onions
  • 1/2 tb and 1/2 tsb all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsb Salt
  • 1 tsb butter
  • Sugar to taste

Steps

  1. In a pot, combine the tomatoes, onions, and chicken brooth. Bring the pot to a boil
  2. Empty the pot and melt the butter with medium heat.
  3. Stir the flour to make a roux until it is medium brown. Then gradually mix in the tomato mixture. Season with salt and sugar to taste.

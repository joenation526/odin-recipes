Mashed potatoes

Description

Simple mashed potatoes that will leave your tastebuds satisfied!

Ingredients

  • 2 large Potatoes (Peeled is optional)
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Half a stick of butter
  • 1 cup of water or chicken stock

Steps

  1. Wash your potatoes
  2. Boil the liquid in a pot and toss in the potatoes
  3. Use a fork to poke the potatoes to see if they're ready. The fork should easily penetrate the potatoes. If not, then give it a few more minutes.
  4. Drain the liquid while leaving the potatoes in the pot
  5. Grab a potato masher (a fork works as well), and start mashing away
  6. Once the potatoes are mashed and still hot. Add the butter, salt, and pepper to taste. Then, stir the ingredients together.
